Who We Are

Manufacturer with the widest range of Laser Printer & Copier consumables in China

Shenzhen ASTA Official Consumable Co.,Ltd. is a professional manufacturer of toner and copier cartridge in China. Founded in 2004, ASTA commit ourself to help promote the global printing technology, it now have more than 300 dedicated employees and nearly 15000 square meters production base. Although factory headquarters located in Shenzhen,China, it has branches and agents established in Dubai,Mexico,Bangladesh,Mongolia,Mali,Kyrghyzstan,Algeria,Iraq,Kuwait,Egypt,Qatar,Nigeria,etc. Cooperative customers spread over 100 countries in world and reach end users more than 200 million.

With the mission “Set up a good example for Chinese Laser Consumables Suppliers”,ASTA strictly comply with its production system. From the incoming raw material testing to outgoing product inspection , 40 more professional QC on duty in whole production procedure.Meanwhile, R&D experts will use High/Low Temperature Storage Equipment to test every product which ordered by extreme temperature districts to ensure life span of the product.

As the manufacturer with the widest range of laser printer and copier consumables in China,ASTA compatible toner and copier cartridges cover HP/CANON/SAMSUNG/XEROX/BROTHER/DELL/LEXMARK/KYOCERA/OKI/EPSON/KONICA MINOLTA/PANASONIC/TOSHIBA/RICOH/LENOVO.More than 2000 models and timely new product update system help ASTA serve customers efficiently.

With the company spirit “One Line One Dream”,ASTA promise to continue provide top quality products with best service to our business partners. Growing up together with partners under the mutual-trust and mutual-understanding relationship. Let printing business cover every corner of the world.
