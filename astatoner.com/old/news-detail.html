The Remax is finished after the 3 days exhibition, this is the 4th Expo in Zhuhai, and almost 200 people which from 13 countries and areas joined in the opening ceremony. There were 350 exhibitors and more than 8000 spectators. With the developing in 4 years, Zhuhai Remax Expo is already the biggest, and most important one in printer consumables filed in the world, and it’s the trading and showing platform for Zhuhai to be the Printer comsumables’ city.

ASTA joined this expo, it indicates that we have taken a strong step to the international market, we showed our ASTA feature’s products and service, received our customers and friends all over the world, then we learn and progess together. And we will keep striving on our goals.with our constant faith “one line, one dream”.
